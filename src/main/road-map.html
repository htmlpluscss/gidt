Road Map

Most affordable, simple and fast way to harness the power
of Decentralized Clouds for Defi and dApps.

Q4 2020

  1. Integration of Arweave / Lambda / SIA Decentralized Storages
  2. Proof of Concept (PoC) developed
  3. POC Live Demo deployed on Rinkeby testnet

Q1 2021

  1. Integration of Filecoin / Storj Decentralized Storages
  2. Launch a private beta of Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
  3. Implement blockchain integration to provide MVP for the marketplace and DeFi

Q2 2021

  1. Integration of extended set of Decentralized Storages
  2. Launch a public beta of MVP (ETH main net)
  3. Extended data storage features for DeFi (PoC)

Q3 2021

  1. Full launch of the product
  2. Implementation of Binance Smart Chain and EOS
  3. Launch of cross-chain bridges (EOS <-> BSC, ETH <-> EOS)
